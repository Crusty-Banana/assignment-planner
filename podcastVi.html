PODCAST

Podcasting is an audio series covering a variety of themes, from academic talks to storytelling and interviews. Podcasting extends beyond the conventional written format. It allows podcast makers or students to go further into their chosen subjects using spoken word, improving their communication abilities and building a stronger bond with the listeners. Researching, writing, producing, and editing a piece of work not only sharpens academic skills but also fosters creativity and flexibility. Additionally, podcasts have a wider audience outside of the classroom, allowing podcast makers to meaningfully participate in public conversation and share their knowledge with a wider audience.
