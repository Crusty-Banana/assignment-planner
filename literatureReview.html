LITERATURE REVIEW

A literature review is carefully reviewing and summarizing previous scholarly works on a given subject or research question. It offers a thorough overview of the level of knowledge at the time on a certain topic. Writers use a literature review to critically study and analyze a variety of sources, including books, journals, and other academic publications, in order to spot trends, gaps, and points of contention. Through this approach, they not only gain a deeper comprehension of the subject, but they are also better able to place their own research within the larger body of previous scholarship. An effective literature review is a crucial part of any academic paper since it provides a foundation for future research.
