POSTER PRESENTATION

A poster presentation is a brief visual summary of research findings or a subject that is generally presented at conferences, seminars, or academic events on a sizable poster board. A strong visual presentation and high-quality studies are both components of an effective poster presentation. A STEM presentation uses text, graphs, charts, and graphics to explain difficult scientific concepts, experimental findings, or ground-breaking technology innovations. STEM poster presentations give researchers, engineers, and scientists opportunities to display their work, discuss ideas, and get peer comments. This promotes collaboration and the sharing of knowledge throughout the STEM community.
