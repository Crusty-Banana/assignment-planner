RESEARCH ESSAYS

A research paper's objective is to gather multiple perspectives, supporting data, and verifiable facts about a subject from books, journals, and interviews, then to interpret the material in writing. It involves the interaction between the writer, readers, and other authors. Scholars and researchers primarily publish their discoveries and advancements in their fields of study through research papers. While a research paper must be finalized in a format that is clear, concise, and well-organized, writing one is rather a difficult task. It includes processes like investigation, logical thinking, evaluating sources, organizing, and writing. These phases do not go in a straight line; instead, the author swings back and forth, and throughout the process, the argument and focus of the paper are developed.
